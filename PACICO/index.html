About Our Website

The website features a chatbot called PACICO, which is derived from the acronym of Pasig Catholic College, meaning "Personal Assistant Companion for Interaction, Counseling and Operation." The chatbot ensures data safety and serves as a help center for guests and students with concerns about the school system. The project aims to enhance the institution's technological influence and use, with automatic functions and fast processes for delivering messages. Inspired by chatbot inventions like SimSimi, Voiceflow, ChatGPT and Facebook Messenger chatbots, PACICO is an actual friend for all PCCians, providing protection and responsiveness.

To try the chatbot, click the icon in the bottom right corner.
